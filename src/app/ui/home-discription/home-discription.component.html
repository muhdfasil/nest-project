Forensic Science

Our forensic department website is a secure and efficient platform designed to manage and store crucial forensic data. It maintains detailed records of individuals connected with forensic cases, including personal information such as names, addresses, and blood groups. This system aids forensic professionals in tracking case-related individuals, ensuring accurate documentation for legal and investigative purposes.
With a strong focus on security and confidentiality, the platform streamlines data management for forensic teams, helping them access, analyze, and manage case-related information efficiently. By integrating advanced search and categorization features, the website ensures quick retrieval of essential details, supporting forensic investigations and legal proceedings.
